$$\dfrac{\partial g_{\vec{x}}}{\partial \vec{x}} - \dfrac{d}{dt}\dfrac{\partial g_{\vec{x}}}{\partial  \vec{\dot x}} = 0$$
$$ \begin{bmatrix}
	\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial x_1} \\[10pt]
	\dfrac{\partial g}{\partial x_2} \\[10pt]
