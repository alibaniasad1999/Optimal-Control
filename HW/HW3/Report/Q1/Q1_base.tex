$z = f(x, y) = y\sin(x+y)-x\sin(x-y)$ \\
Gradient of f(x, y):
$$\vec{\nabla} f = \begin{bmatrix}
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial x} \\[6pt]
	\dfrac{\partial f}{\partial y}
\end{bmatrix} $$
$$\vec{\nabla} f = \begin{bmatrix}
	y \cos(x + y) - \sin(x - y) - x  \cos(x - y) \\
	y  \cos(x + y) + \sin(x + y) + x  \cos(x - y)
\end{bmatrix} $$
