\subsection{Shooting method}
Final time is free so:
$$
\vec{F} = \begin{bmatrix}
	x_1(t_f) - x_{1_f}\\
	x_2(t_f) - x_{2_f}\\
	\left.(\mathcal{H} - h_t)\right\vert_{t_f}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
$$
\vec{y}_{k+1} = \vec{y}_k - \left.\dfrac{\partial \vec{F}}{\partial \vec{y}}
\right\vert_{\vec{y}_k}
\vec{F}(\vec{y}_k)
$$
